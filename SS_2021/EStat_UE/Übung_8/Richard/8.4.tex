\begin{align*}
    f(x \mid \theta)
    =
    \begin{cases}
        \frac{2 x}{\theta}, & 0 < x < \theta \\
        0,                  & \text{otherwise}
    \end{cases},
\end{align*}

with unknown parameter $\theta > 0$.
Find a minimal sufficient static for $\theta$.
